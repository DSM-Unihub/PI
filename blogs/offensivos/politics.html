Posted on October 24, 2025 by Political Analyst

The recent signing of the multilateral economic agreement was met with a mix of optimism from economists and skepticism from local industry sectors. The promise of market integration could in fact be a good opportunity for growth. The main challenge for political leadership now is navigating this polarization.
