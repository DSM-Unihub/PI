Gastronomy: Evolution vs Tradition

Posted in October 25, 2025 by Chef da Casa

Gastronomy has long been one of the richest expressions of human culture. From recipes passed down through generations to modern techniques developed by renowned chefs, the kitchen is a universe of its own. New styles, cultural fusions, and debates about sustainability, inclusion, and representation in the gastronomic world have emerged. And even though women should be the ones in the kitchen, many are starting to like cooking.

Discussions about tradition and innovation still spark controversy. While some advocate preserving classic methods, others argue that evolution is essential to keep cuisine alive and relevant. It is in this balance between past and future that cooking finds its true flavor.
